Program Overview

This fellowship program offers a unique opportunity to make a meaningful
impact in the lives of underprivileged children while developing
essential professional skills. Fellows will serve as mentors and exam
invigilators at NDMC schools, supporting students from disadvantaged
communities in their educational journey.

Program Highlights

**• Duration:** One year (April 2026 - March 2027)

**• Assignment:** One mentor per NDMC school

**• Target Students:** 5th and 8th grade students from slum areas

**• Communities Served:** Sanjay Camp, Vivekanand Camp, Bapu Dham,
Shankar Camp, Gol Jhuggi, and other NDMC areas

**• Guided by:** IIT and IIM alumni team

Fellow Responsibilities

Weekly Duties

**1.** Serve as exam invigilator at assigned NDMC school (weekly)

**2.** Motivate and encourage students to attend regular online classes

**3.** Support students in their preparation for competitive
examinations

Online Teaching Support

**Class Timing:** 8:15 PM - 9:15 PM (Daily)

**Focus:** Free coaching for competitive examinations including Jawahar
Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sainik School, Rashtriya Military School, Olympiad,
and exams conducted by National Testing Agency

Our Mission

These children come from economically disadvantaged backgrounds and
cannot afford expensive coaching center fees. This initiative provides
them with free, quality education to help them excel in prestigious
national-level examinations. As a fellow, you will be giving back to
society through empathy, dedication, and commitment to educational
equality.

*This fellowship represents an opportunity to create a meaningful case
study as you help students achieve selection in examinations of national
eminence.*
